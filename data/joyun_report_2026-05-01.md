The global skincare market, particularly in India and K-beauty, is experiencing rapid growth driven by demand for active ingredients, barrier-focused formulations, and convenient formats. Emerging ingredients like exosomes and continued innovation in PDRN are creating both competitive threats and new opportunities for brands like Joyun.

## Urgent Alert
Joyun must immediately explore integrating PDRN into new product formats, especially toner pads and cleansers, as key K-beauty competitors like Anua and COSRX are rapidly expanding their PDRN offerings in these trending categories, directly impacting Joyun's core ingredient focus and potential market expansion.

- **Centella Asiatica (Cica)** — A staple in 'skin barrier' focused products, known for calming redness, supporting repair, and strengthening compromised skin. - **Peptides** — Gaining traction for anti-aging, hydration, and improving skin elasticity. Increasingly found in moisturizers and specialized treatment pads. _(Joyun: Joyun could explore peptide-rich formulations for new serums or moisturizers, aligning with the longevity and anti-aging positioning, potentially combining with existing actives.)_
- **Niacinamide** — A versatile active for brightening, pore minimization, redness reduction, and barrier function improvement. Indian brands like Plum are launching Niacinamide-focused ranges. _(Joyun: Joyun should consider Niacinamide as a hero ingredient for new product development, especially in cleansers or moisturizers, to address common Indian skin concerns like dullness and uneven tone.)_
