Hot ingredients

Exosomes

Exosomes are rapidly gaining traction in K-beauty and clinical aesthetics, now moving into at-home topical products for advanced cellular repair, anti-aging, collagen/elastin stimulation, and inflammation reduction.

Joyun angle: Joyun could explore exosome-mimicking botanicals or sustainable lab-grown versions to align with its vegan positioning and tap into the 'regenerative beauty' trend, offering cutting-edge cellular repair benefits.

Postbiotics

Postbiotics, the non-living metabolic byproducts of fermentation, are redefining microbiome skincare. They offer stable and effective solutions for skin harmony and resilience, strengthening the skin barrier, boosting hydration, and reducing inflammation, especially for sensitive and compromised skin.

Joyun angle: As a brand focused on vegan mucin and gentle formulations, postbiotics align well with Joyun's 'skin barrier' and 'skin health' focus, offering a stable and scientifically advanced ingredient to enhance existing products or create new ones.

Mushrooms (e.g., Tremella, Reishi, Chaga, Shiitake)

Various mushroom extracts are gaining significant popularity as natural, multi-functional ingredients. They offer hydration (Tremella as 'vegan hyaluronic acid'), soothing, antioxidant, brightening, collagen support, and barrier repair benefits for diverse skin concerns.

Joyun angle: Joyun, with its vegan focus, could leverage mushrooms, especially Tremella, as a powerful hydrating and soothing active, potentially as a 'vegan hyaluronic acid' alternative or complement to its ROSE-03 Vegan Mucin Essence.

Opportunities

Category Gap: Barrier Repair Moisturizers

The 'skin barrier' focus is a dominant trend in 2026, with high demand for products containing ceramides, panthenol, and postbiotics. Many Indian brands are launching affordable options, indicating a ripe market.

Action: Develop a 'ROSE-XX Barrier Repair Cream' or 'Vegan Mucin Barrier Cream' incorporating ceramides, panthenol, and potentially postbiotics, positioned at an accessible price point (e.g., INR 400-600 / ~$4.80-7.20 USD).

Category Gap: Advanced Body Care

Competitors like Foxtale are successfully expanding into body care with facial-grade actives, indicating consumer readiness for sophisticated body treatments that go beyond basic hydration.

Action: Launch a 'ROSE-XX Vegan Mucin Body Essence' or 'PDRN Body Serum' to extend Joyun's core ingredient strengths into a growing category, emphasizing the luxury and efficacy of facial-grade ingredients for the body.

Ingredient Innovation: Exosome-Inspired Formulations

Exosomes are a cutting-edge ingredient for regenerative beauty, cellular repair, and anti-aging, with high consumer interest and a move towards at-home topical applications.

Action: Research and develop a 'ROSE-XX Regenerative Exosome-Mimic Serum' or 'Vegan Exosome Essence,' focusing on plant-derived compounds that offer similar cellular communication and repair benefits to align with Joyun's vegan ethos.

Where Joyun can shine

  • Pioneering Vegan Mucin: Joyun is uniquely positioned with its 'ROSE-03 Vegan Mucin Essence,' offering an ethical and sustainable alternative in a market still largely dominated by snail mucin. Emphasize the cruelty-free and plant-derived benefits.
  • Rose Extract Heritage & Efficacy: Leverage the traditional and scientifically recognized benefits of rose extracts (anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, soothing) as a signature ingredient across its product line, connecting it to natural efficacy and a calming experience.
  • Affordable 'Derm-Luxury' with K-Beauty Influence: Position Joyun as an accessible brand offering K-beauty inspired, science-backed formulations (like PDRN and vegan mucin) with a strong focus on skin health and barrier respect, at competitive Indian price points.
