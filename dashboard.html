Hot ingredients

Exosomes

Emerging as a futuristic, high-tech ingredient for cellular communication, regeneration, anti-aging, and skin repair, primarily in high-end serums and treatments.

Joyun angle: Joyun should monitor the development and consumer acceptance of exosomes as a potential advanced ingredient for future premium anti-aging or repair-focused product lines.

Postbiotics / Fermented Actives

Microbiome science is evolving towards postbiotics and fermentation-derived actives, which strengthen the skin barrier, boost hydration, calm inflammation, and improve elasticity.

Joyun angle: Joyun's vegan mucin could be enhanced or complemented by postbiotics to amplify skin barrier support. This trend aligns with natural and 'skin harmony' positioning.

PDRN

Continues to be a key ingredient for cellular repair and regeneration, with new brands like Medicube launching PDRN lines in India.

Joyun angle: This validates Joyun's ROSE-04 PDRN Micro-Needle Serum. Joyun should emphasize the proven benefits and efficacy of PDRN, potentially exploring new formats or combinations.

Ceramides & Panthenol

These are foundational ingredients for the overarching 'skin barrier care' trend, focusing on proactive maintenance, hydration, and soothing.

Joyun angle: Joyun should consider incorporating these barrier-strengthening ingredients into new product developments, especially in categories like cleansers or moisturizers, to align with a major consumer demand for resilient skin.

Opportunities

Entry into Sunscreen Category

High demand in India for effective, comfortable sunscreens with diverse formulations (mineral, tinted, stick) and benefits (cooling, no white cast, barrier repair) is evident from Dot & Key's aggressive launches and K-beauty innovations.

Action: Develop a Joyun sunscreen line, potentially incorporating rose extracts or vegan mucin for unique positioning, focusing on lightweight, non-greasy, and no-white-cast formulations suitable for Indian weather.

Launch of Barrier-Friendly Cleansers

The 'skin barrier care' trend is foundational, and K-beauty brands are launching gentle, hydrating cleansers that support the microbiome. Cleansers represent a significant category gap for Joyun.

Action: Research and develop a gentle, pH-balanced cleanser, possibly infused with vegan mucin, rose extracts, or ceramides/panthenol, positioned around skin barrier support and suitable for sensitive skin.

Introduction of Toner Pads

The global toner pad market is experiencing robust growth, driven by K-beauty, convenience, and demand for multifunctional, targeted solutions. Medicube's success is a strong indicator.

Action: Explore developing vegan mucin or rose-infused toner pads for specific concerns like hydration, gentle exfoliation, or soothing, leveraging the convenience factor.

Expansion into Body Care

Indian brands like Tint Cosmetics are successfully launching body care products tailored for the local climate, indicating an underserved market for luxurious yet effortless body treatments.

Action: Consider developing a Joyun body care product (e.g., body oil, lotion, serum) that aligns with the brand's 'gentle and effective' ethos, potentially using rose extracts or adaptogens.
