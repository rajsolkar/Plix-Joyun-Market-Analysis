Hot ingredients

PDRN (Polydeoxyribonucleotide)

Moving from clinic-core to mainstream K-beauty, positioned for 'glass skin' results, anti-aging without harsh retinoids, barrier repair, bounce, and plumpness.

Joyun angle: Joyun's ROSE-04 PDRN Micro-Needle Serum is directly relevant. Joyun should emphasize its regenerative and barrier-supporting benefits and explore 'Derm-Luxury' positioning.

Exosomes & Growth-Factor Mimics

High-end science now packaged for retail, focusing on elasticity, collagen appearance, and skin texture improvement.

Joyun angle: Joyun should explore plant-based exosomes as a future innovation to tap into the biotech and regenerative skincare trend.

Centella Asiatica (Cica)

A timeless K-beauty hero, refined with advanced extraction, known for soothing irritation, reducing redness, accelerating skin repair, and strengthening the skin barrier.

Joyun angle: Joyun could incorporate Cica into new soothing, calming, or barrier-focused products, especially given its plant-based and gentle approach.

Fermented Actives/Postbiotics

Evolving from traditional essences to microbiome and skin resilience focus, emphasizing skin balance, reduced irritation, and smoother texture. Postbiotics nurture and balance the microbiome.

Joyun angle: Joyun could explore fermented rose extracts or postbiotic formulations to align with the microbiome-first and sustainable beauty trends.

Adaptogens (e.g., Ashwagandha, Reishi, Tremella)

Helping skin build resilience against environmental aggressors, emotional stress, and inflammation. Tremella is also known as 'vegan hyaluronic acid'.

Joyun angle: Joyun could explore adaptogen-infused products, leveraging its Indian heritage (e.g., Ashwagandha) and the rising demand for stress-resistant skincare.

Vegan Mucin (e.g., Prickly Pear Peptide Mucin, Vegreen Nature Mucin)

Emerging as a popular, ethical alternative to snail mucin, offering hydration, barrier improvement, and a 'juicy skin' effect.

Joyun angle: Joyun's ROSE-03 Vegan Mucin Essence is perfectly positioned. Joyun should strongly highlight its vegan and cruelty-free aspects and the efficacy of its mucin.
