Hot ingredients

Postbiotics / Fermented Actives

A major trend for 2026, focusing on microbiome balance, barrier health, and inflammation reduction, moving beyond live probiotics to stable metabolites.

Joyun angle: Opportunity to incorporate into new products (cleansers, moisturizers) or enhance existing ones, aligning with 'skin barrier' focus and 'fermented beauty' positioning.

Exosomes

Highly buzzed-about for cellular communication, regeneration, collagen synthesis, and anti-inflammatory properties, appearing in high-end topical skincare.

Joyun angle: Consider for future premium serum or essence formulations, aligning with regenerative and 'skin longevity' claims, potentially at a higher price point.

Advanced Peptides

Consistent trend with new generations focusing on collagen production, firmness, repair, and anti-inflammatory benefits, with improved stability and absorption.

Joyun angle: Can be combined with existing rose extracts or vegan mucin for enhanced anti-aging/repair benefits in new serums or creams, offering science-backed efficacy.

Mushrooms (Tremella, Reishi, Chaga)

Trending for hydration, antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and barrier-strengthening properties; Tremella is noted as a hyaluronic acid alternative.

Joyun angle: Opportunity for new hydrating essences, masks, or cleansers, leveraging natural, adaptogenic positioning and aligning with 'clean beauty' trends.

Plant-Based PDRN

Emerging as a vegan and sustainable alternative to salmon-derived PDRN, offering similar repair and barrier-support benefits.

Joyun angle: Direct relevance to ROSE-04; investigate plant-based PDRN for future iterations or new product lines to appeal to conscious consumers and differentiate.

Opportunities

Category Expansion: Affordable Barrier Repair Moisturizer

High consumer demand in India for barrier-focused products across various price points, with local and global brands actively competing.

Action: Develop an affordable, barrier-repair focused moisturizer (cream or gel) incorporating trending ingredients like ceramides, postbiotics, or adaptogens, leveraging Joyun's gentle formulation philosophy.

Category Expansion: Gentle, Hydrating Cleansers

PDRN and other actives are being integrated into cleansers by K-beauty brands, and Joyun's existing ingredients (rose, vegan mucin) are suitable for gentle cleansing.

Action: Launch a gentle, hydrating cleanser featuring rose extracts or vegan mucin, potentially with microbiome-friendly actives, to capture the 'skin barrier first' consumer.

Ingredient Innovation: Plant-Based PDRN

Growing consumer preference for vegan and sustainable options, with plant-based PDRN alternatives emerging in the market.

Action: Research and develop a plant-based PDRN alternative for ROSE-04 or a new PDRN product line to appeal to conscious consumers and differentiate from salmon-derived PDRN.

Format Innovation: Essence Toner Pads or Mists

K-beauty brands are leveraging convenient formats like toner pads and mists for active delivery and on-the-go application.

Action: Explore developing toner pads or mists with Joyun's existing rose/vegan mucin essences or new formulations, offering convenience and targeted application for daily 'barrier care'.
