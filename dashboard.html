Hot ingredients

Exosomes

Rapidly becoming a cornerstone of regenerative skincare, moving from in-clinic treatments to advanced home care formulations, with online searches peaking at over 500k/month.

Joyun angle: Joyun can explore exosome-inspired technology to enhance the regenerative benefits of its PDRN serum or develop new anti-aging products, aligning with the 'skin longevity' trend.

Postbiotics

Highly stable and effective in strengthening the skin barrier, retaining moisture, reducing signs of aging, and soothing irritation, making them ideal for sensitive skin.

Joyun angle: Joyun can integrate postbiotics into new formulations or enhance existing products to boost skin barrier support and cater to the growing demand for microbiome-friendly skincare.

Mushrooms (Snow/Tremella, Reishi, Chaga)

Trending for their multitasking benefits, including deep hydration (Snow Mushroom acts like 'vegan hyaluronic acid'), soothing irritated skin, and supporting the skin barrier.

Joyun angle: Joyun, with its vegan ethos, can explore mushroom extracts like Snow Mushroom for enhanced hydration and barrier repair in new essences or serums, aligning with sustainable and plant-based trends.

Peptides

Next-generation peptides are cornerstones of regenerative medicine and advanced home care, often used in combination with other actives for anti-aging and skin firming.

Joyun angle: Joyun can consider incorporating a broader range of peptides to boost the anti-aging and firming claims of its ROSE-04 PDRN Micro-Needle Serum or develop a dedicated peptide-rich product.

Fermented Actives

Making a significant comeback in K-beauty and gaining traction in India, these ingredients enhance potency, absorption, and support a healthy skin microbiome, contributing to barrier health.

Joyun angle: Joyun can explore fermented rose extracts or other plant-based ferments to enhance the efficacy and 'skin barrier' positioning of its products, aligning with both K-beauty and traditional Indian beauty influences.
