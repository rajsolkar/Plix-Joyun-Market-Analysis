Peptides

Evolving into more sophisticated, targeted blends for collagen stimulation, barrier health, elasticity, and repair, with copper peptides specifically highlighted.

Joyun angle: Opportunity to enhance ROSE-04 PDRN Micro-Needle Serum with advanced peptide complexes or develop new peptide-focused anti-aging serums to broaden the anti-aging portfolio.

Postbiotics / Fermented Actives

Crucial for microbiome balance, skin barrier strength, hydration, and inflammation reduction, appearing in essences, mists, and creams.

Joyun angle: Integrate into existing lines for enhanced barrier support or develop new microbiome-friendly products, aligning with the 'skin longevity' trend.

Vegan Mucin

A plant-powered, cruelty-free alternative to snail mucin, experiencing strong market growth and numerous new product launches in India.

Joyun angle: This is a core ingredient for Joyun (ROSE-03); differentiation, innovation, and competitive pricing strategies are critical given the increasing competition.

PDRN

Continues to be a leading biostimulatory treatment for deep skin regeneration, improved elasticity, and repair, often combined with peptides and exosomes.

Joyun angle: Joyun's ROSE-04 already leverages PDRN; explore synergistic combinations with emerging ingredients like exosomes and advanced peptides to enhance efficacy and market appeal.

Opportunities

Dedicated 'Skin Barrier Repair' Line

There is a strong market trend and high consumer demand in India for products focused on strengthening and repairing the skin barrier.

Action: Formulate a barrier-focused moisturizer or serum, clearly highlighting barrier-strengthening ingredients like ceramides, phytosphingosine, or postbiotics, leveraging Joyun's vegan and soothing properties.

Overnight Masks / Sleeping Masks

Overnight masks are a popular and growing format in India for deep hydration and repair, with several Indian and K-beauty brands launching products.

Action: Introduce an overnight mask, potentially featuring Joyun's vegan mucin, PDRN, or rose extracts, focusing on a non-sticky, deeply hydrating formula for Indian climates.

Body Care Expansion with High-Performing Actives

High-performing facial skincare ingredients like retinoids, peptides, exosomes, and even snail mucin are increasingly moving into body care products.

Action: Explore body care products infused with Joyun's signature ingredients (vegan mucin, PDRN, rose extracts) to tap into this emerging category gap, such as a vegan mucin body lotion or a PDRN-infused body serum.

Integration of Postbiotics/Fermented Actives

Postbiotics and fermented actives are key to the 'skin longevity' and 'microbiome-first' trends, offering benefits for barrier strength, hydration, and inflammation.

Action: Begin R&D to incorporate postbiotic ferments into existing or new Joyun formulations, particularly in essences or mists, to align with advanced microbiome science.
