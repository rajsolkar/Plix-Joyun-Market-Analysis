Hot ingredients

Exosomes

Emerging as a high-efficacy ingredient for cellular repair, anti-aging, collagen production, and reducing inflammation, found in premium serums and treatments. Plant-based exosomes are also gaining traction.

Joyun angle: Joyun could explore plant-based exosomes for a premium, high-efficacy line, aligning with its vegan ethos and offering advanced anti-aging benefits.

Postbiotics

Beneficial by-products of probiotics, offering greater stability and proven benefits for microbiome balance, skin barrier reinforcement, hydration, and soothing sensitive skin.

Joyun angle: Joyun can integrate postbiotics into new product formulations, especially for cleansers or moisturizers, to enhance skin barrier support and cater to the growing sensitive skin segment.

Centella Asiatica (Cica)

A timeless K-beauty hero, with mentions up significantly, known for its calming, redness-reducing, and skin-repairing properties, central to the skin barrier repair trend.

Joyun angle: Joyun could introduce Centella into a new soothing line or combine it with existing rose extracts to create a powerful calming and barrier-supporting range.

Snow Mushroom (Tremella fuciformis)

Positioned as 'vegan hyaluronic acid' in K-beauty, offering sustainable hydration and anti-aging benefits, aligning with ethical and eco-conscious beauty.

Joyun angle: Joyun, with its vegan mucin focus, could explore Snow Mushroom as a complementary or alternative hydrating ingredient to reinforce its vegan and sustainable positioning.

Opportunities

Category Gap: Toner Pads

Toner pads are a highly popular and convenient format in K-beauty and are gaining significant traction in the Indian market, offering multi-benefit solutions (e.g., soothing, exfoliating, hydrating).

Action: Develop a Joyun-branded toner pad line, potentially featuring Vegan Mucin for soothing/hydration or PDRN for targeted repair, leveraging Joyun's existing hero ingredients.

Category Gap: Gentle Cleansers

Both J-beauty and K-beauty trends emphasize gentle, barrier-friendly cleansers as the foundational step in a 'skin barrier first' routine. Joyun currently lacks a cleanser offering.

Action: Launch a 'ROSE-01 Gentle Cleansing Balm' or 'ROSE-02 Vegan Mucin Cleansing Foam' focused on maintaining skin barrier integrity and soothing properties.

Positioning Trend: Skin Barrier Repair & Longevity

The 'skin barrier focus' is the most significant overarching trend for 2026, with consumers prioritizing long-term skin health, resilience, and gentle formulations over aggressive treatments.

Action: Reposition existing ROSE-03 and ROSE-04 products to explicitly highlight their barrier-supporting and skin-strengthening benefits. Develop new products specifically formulated with ceramides, postbiotics, or adaptogens to target barrier repair.

Where Joyun can shine

  • Leveraging its unique 'Vegan Mucin' and 'PDRN' as ethical, high-efficacy biotech ingredients, positioning them within the broader 'skin longevity' and 'intentional maximalism' trends for discerning consumers.
  • Emphasizing its Indian origin while strategically incorporating globally trending K-beauty/J-beauty inspired ingredient science and format innovations, creating a 'modern Indian skincare' identity.
  • Highlighting 'rose extracts' as a signature, calming, and antioxidant-rich botanical, pairing it with advanced actives to offer a unique 'modern Ayurveda' appeal that resonates with both traditional and contemporary preferences.
